Our Vision

• True to its name Karunya which means 'Compassion' shall be an institution
with social concern to address the problems of humanity through technical
education, research and development, products, alumins ,patents and extension.

• The faculty and students of Karunya will be nurtured in character, ethics and
spiritual discernment to serve the society with fervor and zeal.
